## Notes on Data Quality

- **Population figures** use 2021 CBS Nepal census as baseline
- **Economic anchors** and **development possibilities** fields are analytical assessments by the researcher, not official government classifications
- **District budget** data at local government level is difficult to obtain centrally — recommendations are cross-referenced with sector-level federal budget allocations
- **Market pricing** reflects 2025–2026 field reports and industry sources; prices fluctuate seasonally
- **Constituency-level data** is estimated from district-level data; micro-planning should be validated at ward/municipality level with local authorities
